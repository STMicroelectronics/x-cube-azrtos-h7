 Led control

This page allows you to control LED1 located in the STM32H735G-DK Discovery board. To turn on/off green led you have to check corresponding radio button. Then you have to click on "Send" button to submit the new LED configuration. Finally check in the  STM32H735G-DK-Discovery board that you get the green LED is putted on/off.
